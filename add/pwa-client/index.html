How to Connect Devices

Three ways to connect — choose the one that fits your setup.
Method 1 Same Browser — Two Tabs
1. Generate identity in this tab
2. Click Open 2nd Tab in the chat bar
3. Generate a different identity in the new tab
4. Both tabs auto-discover each other via BroadcastChannel
5. Messages are fully encrypted between tabs
Method 2 Two Devices — Manual Key Exchange
1. Generate identity on Device A → click View Keys
2. Copy the Public Key and send it to Device B (any method)
3. On Device B → click + Add → paste Device A's public key
4. Repeat in reverse (B's key → A)
5. Messages are now encrypted end-to-end
Method 3 QR Code Exchange
1. Generate identity → click Share QR
2. The other device goes to ⊞ QR → pastes your QR JSON
3. Repeat in reverse
4. Encryption is now active
Method 4 Web Bluetooth (Chrome on Android/Desktop)
1. Both devices must advertise as "MeshComm" GATT service
2. Click ⟁ BT Scan to discover nearby devices
3. Keys are exchanged automatically on connect
Note: requires Chrome + Bluetooth hardware + GATT server running
Where to find your Public Key
→ Click ⚙ My Keys (top right) or View Keys (sidebar)
→ Your public key is the 44-char base64 string under Ed25519 Public Key
→ Share it freely — it cannot be used to decrypt your messages
